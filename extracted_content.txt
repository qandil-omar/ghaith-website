More Than a Factory — We Are Egypt's Agricultural Legacy
Sub-headline:
Since 2014, ALGHAITH has been turning Egypt's finest harvests into world-class products that reach homes, restaurants, and supermarkets across continents.
Our
Story
 — 
قصتنا
ALGHAITH Factory was born from a deep belief in two things: the unmatched richness of Egyptian soil, and the world's need for honest, high-quality food.
Founded in 2014 in Alatwa Alqeblia, Qutour, Al Gharbia — a governorate historically known as the backbone of Egypt's agricultural output — our factory was built from the ground up with a single purpose: to process and preserve Egypt's finest fruits and vegetables while maintaining every gram of their natural value, flavor, and nutrition.
Over the years, we have grown from a regional processor into a globally recognized exporter, earning certifications from the world's most demanding food safety authorities: the U.S. FDA, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018, Kosher (World of Kashrut), and HALAL (IFNAS). These are not logos on a page — they are a daily commitment we make to every partner who trusts us with their supply chain.
Today, ALGHAITH serves leading retailers, food manufacturers, and private-label brands in the Gulf, Europe, and the Americas. Our eight product categories span frozen vegetables and fruits, canned legumes, olives, stuffed vegetables, artichokes, pickles, and roasted peppers — all processed with advanced IQF and canning technology in our state-of-the-art facility.
We believe that quality is not a feature. It is the foundation of everything we do.

IQF-Frozen at the Peak of Freshness — Zero Compromise on Quality
مقدمة
:
Our frozen vegetable line is built on one technology: IQF — Individually Quick Frozen. Unlike conventional block-freezing, IQF encases each individual piece in a shell of ice within seconds of harvest, locking in natural color, texture, and nutritional density that slower methods destroy. Every vegetable in this range is sourced directly from Al Gharbia farms, processed within hours of harvest, and frozen to a standard that top-tier food manufacturers, retailers, and catering chains demand globally.

:
The Difference Is in the Details — And the Details Are in Every Product
مقدمة
:
In a market crowded with food processors and exporters, what truly separates a good supplier from a great one? At ALGHAITH, we believe the answer is three things: the quality of your raw materials, the integrity of your process, and the reliability of your commitment to partners. We deliver on all three — every time, every shipment.
1 — Farm-to-Factory Control
Unlike many processors who rely on open-market purchasing, ALGHAITH works directly with a network of vetted, trusted farms in Al Gharbia Governorate and across the Nile Delta. This direct relationship gives us full visibility and control over growing conditions, pesticide use, harvest timing, and produce quality — long before anything enters our processing lines.
The result: raw materials that are consistently superior, and finished products that taste and perform better than the competition.
2 — Advanced IQF &amp; Canning Technology
We have invested significantly in state-of-the-art IQF tunnel freezers, automated blanching systems, modern canning lines, and glass jar filling equipment. These are not just machines — they are the reason our products maintain their natural color, texture, and nutritional density long after leaving our facility.
IQF freezing means each piece freezes individually in under two minutes, preserving cell structure and preventing the clumping and texture loss associated with conventional block freezing. It is the global gold standard, and it is the standard we apply daily.

We hold five internationally recognized certifications: U.S. FDA, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018, Kosher (World of Kashrut), and HALAL (IFNAS). These are the result of rigorous third-party audits of our facility, processes, documentation, and food safety management systems.

What is your minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities vary by product category. For frozen products, we work from a minimum of 1 pallet (approximately 500–1,000 kg). For canned goods, minimums start at 1 full pallet per SKU. Contact us with your specific requirements for a tailored quote.
Do you offer private label services?
Yes. We offer full private label manufacturing across all eight of our product categories — in cans, glass jars, and frozen bulk/retail formats. Contact us to discuss your brand requirements and we will prepare a proposal within 48 hours.
What certifications do your products hold?
Our facility holds five international certifications: U.S. FDA (Reg. No. 19951687376), ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018, Kosher (World of Kashrut), and HALAL (IFNAS). All certificates are available for download from our Certifications page or on request.
Which markets do you currently export to?
We currently export to Gulf Cooperation Council countries, Europe, and the United States. We welcome enquiries from distributors and importers in all regions.
Can I visit your factory?
Absolutely. We welcome factory visits from serious partners and prospective buyers. Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit and we will arrange a comprehensive facility tour.
What packaging options are available?
We offer: 400g retail cans, A9 catering cans (2.65 kg), A10 bulk cans (3 kg), glass jars (180ml to 2L), and frozen retail bags (400g–1 kg) and bulk cartons (5–10 kg). Custom sizes are available for private label orders.
How long does it take to receive a price quotation?
We respond to all pricing enquiries within 24 business hours. For private label or custom product requests, we typically provide a full proposal within 3–5 business days.
